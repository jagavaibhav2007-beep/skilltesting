# SOHub — Premium Design Analysis & Strategy

This document outlines the visual language, typography, and structural patterns identified on `sohub.digital` to be implemented in the portfolio project.

## 1. Typography System (Geometric Brutalism)

The core impact of SOHub comes from its "Monumental" typography.

*   **Primary Font**: General Sans or Satoshi (Geometric Neo-grotesk).
    *   *Implementation Choice*: **Outfit** (Google Fonts) for best matching of geometric curves and tracking flexibility.
*   **Scale**: 
    *   **Hero**: `10vw` or more. Oversized headers that often crop or overlap.
    *   **Section Titles**: Large but tight.
*   **Tracking (Letter-Spacing)**: 
    *   Headlines: **`-0.05em`** (Extreme negative tracking creates a unified "block" look).
    *   CTAs: **`0.1em`** (Wide tracking for utility elements).
*   **Leading (Line-Height)**: 
    *   Hero: **`0.85` to `0.9`**. Lines are packed so tightly they almost touch, giving an architectural feel.

## 2. Layout Patterns

*   **Bento Grids**: Asymmetrical grid systems used for expertise and capabilities.
*   **Sticky Stacking**: Cards that stick to the top of the viewport while the next card slides over it (Z-index layering).
*   **High Contrast**: Pure backgrounds (#000000 or #0d0d10) with vibrant accents or soft white glows.
*   **Monochromatic Base**: 90% Zinc/Black/White with focused accent pops (e.g., Electric Blue or Emerald).

## 3. Visual Depth (Glass & 3D)

*   **Glassmorphism**: Thick frosted glass (`blur-2xl`) with micro-thin white borders (`border-white/10`).
*   **3D Presence**: Elements don't just fade; they rise on the Z-axis. Use of `perspective` and `scale`.
*   **Ambient Glow**: Large, soft radial gradients in the background to prevent "flatness" in dark mode.

## 4. Interaction Archetypes

*   **Physical Hover**: Cards should feel like physical objects that lift or tilt when interacted with.
*   **Smooth Scroll**: Parallax offsets on background text (e.g., "STACK" or "WORK").
*   **Terminal Micro-copy**: Use of monospaced system status indicators to add an engineering/technical layer to the aesthetics.
